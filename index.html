BEAVER MARINE CONSTRUCTION

Beaver Marine offers low maintenance, heavy framed aluminum swim docks, gangways, residential & commercial boat docks. We deliver a boat dock that not only enchances the appearance & value of your waterfront property but is engineered to withstand the continuous & extreme exposure of marine conditions. Our partnership with Wahoo Docks, one of the largest dock manufacturers in North America, enables Beaver Marine to offer what has been recognized as the elite docking system for the past 20 years.

CONCRETE DOCKS

The engineering focus, efficiency gains & design optimizations at Wahoo Docks combined with Beaver Marine' proficiency in regional codes, regulations, & installation techniques delivers a local approach to a fully engineered dock system that is tailored to your specific needs.
